 Impact Statements: Ambulatory Surgery Centers  

IMMEDIATE

Immediate impacts to patient care include:

  • Rescheduling surgeries to another ASC 1a. Longer wait times to schedule surgeries
  • Patient re-prioritizing in another ASC or hospital
  • No notice event will turn it into a hospital need to evacuate
  • Notice won't be performing and can become surge space

DOWNSTREAM

Closure of these providers will impact thesee other providers in the following way:

  • Delaying or putting off surgeries/procedures
  • Increase in costs incurred by the hospital if elected procedures are completed in a hospital operating room

LONG-TERM

Closure of these providers will impact communities in the long-term in the following ways:

  • Increased morbidity
  • Increased demand on hospital
  • Long waiting period
  • Delivery of care
  • Divergent of care to other facilities and urgent centers can increase costs
